HAIRby Julie

Hair by Julie is the oldest unisex hairdresser in Dufftown. Julie herself has owned the salon for 20 years. We pride ourselves on our friendly nature and walk-in approach.

Just pop along and we can make a space for you whether you want your hair styled or to buy one of our products. If you’d rather have an appointment in the comfort of your own home we can arrange a home visit where one of our employees will come to you.

Hair by Julie is a part of the Hairdressers’ Federation and we receive the Hairdressers’ Journal in order to keep up with the latest trends.
